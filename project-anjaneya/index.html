Diagnostic Services

What We Offer

A comprehensive range of ultrasonography and imaging services under expert supervision.

🤰

Obstetric Ultrasound

Full pregnancy scans — dating, anomaly, growth, and Doppler studies for mother and baby health.

🫀

Abdominal Ultrasound

Detailed imaging of liver, gallbladder, kidneys, spleen, pancreas, and abdominal vessels.

🫁

Pelvic Ultrasound

Evaluation of uterus, ovaries, bladder, and prostate for gynaecological and urological concerns.

🩺

Thyroid & Neck USG

High-resolution imaging of thyroid gland, lymph nodes, and soft tissues of the neck.

📡

Breast Ultrasound

Early detection of cysts, masses, and tissue abnormalities — complementary to mammography.

🦴

Musculoskeletal USG

Precise imaging of joints, tendons, ligaments, and soft tissues for injury and inflammation.

🧪

USG-Guided Procedures

Accurate guided biopsies and aspirations under real-time ultrasound visualization.
